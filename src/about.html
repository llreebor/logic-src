Our Story

We’ve solved the hardest AI challenges for small startups to Fortune 100 companies for years by leveraging the best data science platforms available. In the early days, clients asked one question, "could you model it to do…?" But there were a lot of unanswered questions too. Questions like:

  • Can you prepare data so the model results aren't misleading?
  • How do you know you’ve built the best AI model?
  • How easily can we deploy an AI model built by you?
  • Will your predictions match what we have in mind? How can we be sure?
  • How will we know when a model should be replaced? Do you monitor your AI?
  • How will you optimize the AI for our specific situation?

We wanted to answer these questions too, so we brought together a team of hand-picked experts to create the most advanced AI platform in existence, LogicPlum. Today, our core focus is on developing the best products and embedding AI into business applications that support your highest business ambitions in ways that make sense for you.
